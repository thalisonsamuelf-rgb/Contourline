# Daily Email Quality Checklist

## Overview

Quality gate for daily emails (infotainment-style) generated by the Copy Squad. These emails blend entertainment, personality, and value with a subtle sell. Distinct from promotional emails.

---

## Critical Checks (must pass ALL)

### Infotainment Ratio
- [ ] At least 80% of the email delivers genuine value or entertainment?
- [ ] Reader would enjoy the email even if they never buy?
- [ ] Teaching, insight, or story comes BEFORE any pitch?
- [ ] Pitch is woven into the narrative (not tacked on at the end)?

### Personality Injection
- [ ] Sender's unique voice is unmistakable from line one?
- [ ] Contains at least one opinion, hot take, or personal stance?
- [ ] Humor, irreverence, or vulnerability is present (human tone)?
- [ ] Reader feels like they are hearing from a PERSON, not a brand?

### CTA Subtlety
- [ ] CTA flows naturally from the content (not a jarring pivot)?
- [ ] Link or offer is contextually relevant to the day's story?
- [ ] Sell is embedded, not announced ("By the way..." not "BUY NOW")?
- [ ] Non-buyers still feel respected and valued?

### Open Loop
- [ ] Email ends with a tease or question that makes tomorrow's email anticipated?
- [ ] Callback to a previous email or running thread exists?
- [ ] Reader has a reason to open the NEXT email?

### Unsubscribe-Proof Value
- [ ] If someone only read this email, they got something useful?
- [ ] Email would survive the "would I forward this to a friend?" test?
- [ ] Not a pure pitch with a story wrapper?

## Red Flags (auto-fail if present)

- Email is 90%+ pitch with a thin story veneer
- No personality — could have been written by anyone
- Opens with "I hope this email finds you well" or equivalent
- No value for non-buyers (pure sales push)
- Wall of text with no formatting or visual breaks
- Subject line is clickbait with no payoff in the body
- No open loop or forward momentum to the next email

## Daily Email Technical Checks

- [ ] Word count is 300-700 words (sweet spot for daily)?
- [ ] Single-column format optimized for mobile?
- [ ] No images that distract from the text-based intimacy?
- [ ] From line uses personal name (not company name)?
- [ ] Sent at a consistent time for habit formation?

Total >= 35/50 for approval (70% threshold)
