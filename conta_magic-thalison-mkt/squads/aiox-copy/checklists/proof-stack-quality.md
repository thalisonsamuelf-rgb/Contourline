# Proof Stack Quality Checklist

## Overview

Quality gate for proof stacks generated by the Copy Squad. Validates the variety, sequencing, and credibility escalation of proof elements used in any sales asset.

---

## Critical Checks (must pass ALL)

### Proof Variety
- [ ] At least 3 different proof types are present?
- [ ] Statistical proof (numbers, percentages, data)?
- [ ] Testimonial proof (real people with real results)?
- [ ] Case study proof (detailed before/after stories)?
- [ ] Credential proof (qualifications, media, partnerships)?
- [ ] Demonstration proof (show, don't tell)?

### Sequencing
- [ ] Proof elements build in intensity (not peak early)?
- [ ] First proof element is accessible and relatable?
- [ ] Strongest proof is positioned near the offer/CTA?
- [ ] Proof types alternate (not 5 testimonials in a row)?
- [ ] Each proof addresses a different objection or claim?

### Credibility Escalation
- [ ] Progression moves from peer-level to authority-level proof?
- [ ] Third-party validation (press, certifications) is included?
- [ ] Expert endorsements are specific (not vague praise)?
- [ ] Results escalate from small wins to transformative outcomes?
- [ ] Final proof element is the most impressive or undeniable?

### Specificity
- [ ] Every testimonial includes specific details (numbers, timeframes)?
- [ ] Case studies include before state, after state, and method?
- [ ] Statistics have sources or context?
- [ ] Credentials are verifiable?
- [ ] No generic praise ("It was great!" with no detail)?

---

## Red Flags (auto-fail if present)

- Only one type of proof used (all testimonials, no data)
- Testimonials are vague ("Changed my life!" with no specifics)
- No third-party or independent validation
- Proof feels cherry-picked or too good to be true
- Statistics are unsourced or misleading
- Names and photos appear fake or stock
- Best proof is buried where no one will read it

## Proof Type Reference

| Proof Type | Example | Strength |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Testimonial** | "I lost 30 lbs in 90 days" - Jane, Ohio | Relatable |
| **Case Study** | Full story with timeline and method | Deep trust |
| **Statistic** | "87% of users see results in 14 days" | Logical |
| **Credential** | "Featured in Forbes, NYT, CNN" | Authority |
| **Demonstration** | Live demo, screenshot, before/after photo | Undeniable |
| **Social Proof** | "50,000+ customers in 30 countries" | Bandwagon |
| **Expert Endorsement** | "Dr. Smith recommends this approach" | Authority |
