## Core Principle

The ad does not close the sale. The ad filters, qualifies, and creates the next step.

## Main Formats

### 1. Application Ad

- Best for warm or qualified audiences
- Destination: application form
- Core structure:
  - selective hook
  - qualification
  - specific promise
  - mechanism
  - proof
  - selective CTA

### 2. Story Ad

- Best for cold to warm audiences
- Destination: direct message or application page
- Core structure:
  - emotional hook
  - relatable journey
  - turning point
  - bridge to next step

### 3. Authority Ad

- Best for audience quality and long-term trust building
- Destination: engagement and later funnel movement
- Core structure:
  - contrarian truth or clear observation
  - proof of depth
  - implied authority
  - soft next step

## Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Offer is structured
- [ ] Mechanism is clear
- [ ] Proof exists
- [ ] Application path exists
- [ ] Budget and test volume are realistic

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not use the ad as a full sales letter.
- Do not teach dense technical content in the ad.
- Do not use vague hype without a concrete next step.
- Use the creative to qualify who should continue.

## Performance Lens

Track:
- click-through quality
- application rate
- show rate
- downstream conversion quality

Optimize for qualified intent, not vanity metrics.
