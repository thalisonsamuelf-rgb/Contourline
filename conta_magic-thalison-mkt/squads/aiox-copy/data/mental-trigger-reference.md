## Foundation Triggers

- scarcity
- urgency
- authority
- social proof
- reciprocity
- commitment and consistency
- affinity

- exclusivity

- community

- discovery
- belonging
- validity

## Minimum Baseline

Every serious conversion piece should usually contain at least:

- authority
- social proof
- curiosity
- story
- pain or fear
- reciprocity
- either urgency or scarcity

## Usage Rule

Triggers support the argument. They should not replace specificity, proof, or positioning.
