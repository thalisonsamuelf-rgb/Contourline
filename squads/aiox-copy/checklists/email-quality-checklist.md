# Email Copy Quality Checklist

## Overview

Quality gate for email copy generated by the Copy Squad. Validates individual emails and email sequences before sending.

---

## Critical Checks (must pass ALL)

### Subject Line
- [ ] Under 60 characters (ideally under 45)?
- [ ] Creates curiosity, urgency, or specific benefit?
- [ ] Avoids spam trigger words (FREE, ACT NOW, !!!)?
- [ ] Would stand out in a crowded inbox?
- [ ] Preview text complements (not repeats) subject line?

### Opening Hook
- [ ] First sentence earns the second sentence?
- [ ] No throat-clearing ("I wanted to reach out...", "Hope you're well...")?
- [ ] Creates an open loop or pattern interrupt?
- [ ] Reader knows within 2 lines why this matters to them?

### Story Bridge
- [ ] Narrative connects the hook to the offer naturally?
- [ ] Story is relatable to the target audience?
- [ ] Includes sensory or emotional details (not abstract)?
- [ ] Transition from story to pitch feels seamless?

### CTA
- [ ] One primary CTA (not multiple competing actions)?
- [ ] CTA link/button appears at least twice?
- [ ] Action language is specific ("Get your copy" not "Click here")?
- [ ] CTA is visible without excessive scrolling?

### PS Section
- [ ] PS is present (highest-read section after subject)?
- [ ] PS adds a new reason to act (not just repeats CTA)?
- [ ] PS creates urgency, restates guarantee, or adds social proof?

---

## Red Flags (auto-fail if present)

- No PS section
- Generic subject line that could be from any sender
- Wall of text (paragraphs over 4 lines on mobile)
- Multiple competing CTAs in a single email
- No story or narrative element (pure pitch)
- Sender voice is inconsistent with brand
- Unsubscribe-worthy content (pure sell, no value)

## Email-Specific Technical Checks

- [ ] No broken or placeholder links?
- [ ] Images have alt text (many clients block images)?
- [ ] Renders correctly in plain text mode?
- [ ] Personalization tokens ({{first_name}}) are properly set?
- [ ] From name is recognizable to the recipient?

## Minimum Score

Total >= 35/50 for approval (70% threshold)
