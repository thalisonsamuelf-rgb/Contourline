# Landing Page Quality Checklist

## Overview

Quality gate for landing page copy generated by the Copy Squad. Validates above-fold clarity, scroll motivation, proof placement, CTA visibility, and mobile experience.

---

## Critical Checks (must pass ALL)

### Above-Fold Clarity
- [ ] Headline communicates the primary benefit within 3 seconds?
- [ ] Sub-headline adds specificity or context to the headline?
- [ ] Visitor knows WHO this is for, WHAT they get, and WHY it matters?
- [ ] Primary CTA is visible without scrolling?
- [ ] No jargon, acronyms, or insider language?
- [ ] Visual hierarchy guides the eye: headline > sub-headline > CTA?

### Scroll Motivation
- [ ] Something below the fold is teased or hinted at above?
- [ ] First scroll reveals new, compelling information?
- [ ] Each section earns the next scroll?
- [ ] Content structure follows a logical persuasion sequence?
- [ ] No "dead zones" where attention drops?

### Proof Placement
- [ ] Social proof appears within the first 2 scroll depths?
- [ ] Logos, testimonials, or numbers are positioned near CTAs?
- [ ] Proof variety is used (not just testimonials)?
- [ ] Proof is relevant to the specific claim it supports?
- [ ] At least one proof element with a photo and full name?

### CTA Visibility
- [ ] Primary CTA appears at least 3 times on the page?
- [ ] CTA button text is action-oriented and benefit-driven?
- [ ] CTA contrasts visually with surrounding elements?
- [ ] Every CTA leads to the same action (no competing goals)?
- [ ] Final CTA section includes urgency or a closing argument?

### Mobile Experience
- [ ] Text is readable without zooming (minimum 16px)?
- [ ] CTA buttons are thumb-friendly (minimum 44x44px touch target)?
- [ ] Images scale properly and don't break layout?
- [ ] Form fields (if any) are mobile-optimized?
- [ ] Page loads under 3 seconds on mobile?

## Red Flags (auto-fail if present)

- Above-fold area is unclear about what the page offers
- No CTA visible until deep scroll
- Multiple competing CTAs (subscribe AND buy AND download)
- Zero social proof on the entire page
- Wall of text with no visual hierarchy
- Navigation links that take visitors away from the conversion goal
- Slow load time (images not optimized, heavy scripts)
- Form has more than 3-4 fields for a lead gen page

## Landing Page Type Variations

### Lead Generation (opt-in)
- [ ] Form is above the fold?
- [ ] Lead magnet value is immediately clear?
- [ ] Fields are minimal (name + email maximum for cold traffic)?
- [ ] Privacy note is present near the form?

### Sales Page (long-form)
- [ ] Follows problem > solution > proof > offer > CTA arc?
- [ ] FAQ section addresses top objections?
- [ ] Guarantee section is prominent and specific?
- [ ] Multiple CTA placements throughout the page?

### Webinar/Event Registration
- [ ] Date/time is prominent and clear (with timezone)?
- [ ] 3-5 bullets of what they will learn?
- [ ] Speaker credentials are displayed?
- [ ] Registration form is simple (name + email)?

## The 5-Second Test

Show the landing page to someone for 5 seconds. They must answer:

| Question | Answered Correctly? |
|----------|-------------------|
| What does this page offer? | |
| Who is it for? | |
| What should I do next? | |

3/3 must be correct.
