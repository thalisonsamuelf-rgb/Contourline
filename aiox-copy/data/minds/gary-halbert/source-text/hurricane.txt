The Gary Halbert Letter

Dear Friend & Subscriber,

This may be the most important e-mail you will ever

receive.

At least from me.

I haven’t written a newsletter for nearly two months. This

has caused some people to wonder if I am sick or something. I am not. I feel

great and, as far as I know, I enjoy almost perfect health.

But, the entire state of Florida is a disaster area. I’ve

just taken a road trip here of nearly 1,000 miles. You wouldn’t believe the

damage I’ve seen. Thousands of people have lost everything.

So, I’ve decided to give a FREE teleseminar and give away

at least 90-minutes of hard-core marketing secrets. You don’t have to pay a dime

to be on this teleseminar. But what I am hoping, of course, is you will find the

teleseminar so valuable you will be moved to write a check to the Red Cross

Hurricane Relief Fund.

Right now, I’m in the process of choosing a teleseminar

service and I need to know at least approximately how many people will be on the

conference call.

So, here’s what I’m asking you to do right now. If you are

interested in this teleseminar, please send me an e-mail immediately that just

says, “I’m interested” in the subject line. Send your e-mail to me at…

[email protected]

That won’t obligate you to be

on the call. But, I’ll take it as an indication you might be on it and thus I

can make an intelligent choice when it comes to hiring a teleconference service.

I’ll

write soon giving you all the details. But, for now, do me a small favor and let

me know if you have any interest whatsoever of being on the call.

Sincerely,

Gary C. Halbert
