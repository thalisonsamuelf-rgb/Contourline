Closure | Newsletter

Hi Friends, It’s Kev Rogers here.

Access to Copy Chief is currently closed.

We had an epic eleven year run. I am forever grateful to every person who contributed to this one-of-a-kind community, as a member, coach, client, or an ally.

It’s no exaggeration to say that many lives were changed for the better here in Copy Chief. None more than mine and my family’s.

That wasn’t me who did that, that’s something we all did for each other. I’ve always said my favorite thing about copywriting is the spirit of generosity among the whackadoos who fall in love with this craft. We never hesitate to reach a hand to those on their way up, just as so many pulled us up along the way.

That is a rare–and very human–gift. So, no matter where you are on your journey today, give help wherever you can. It will serve you just as well (quite possibly more) as it will those who receive it.

And hey… who’s to say we won’t be back in a new way at some time. I certainly don’t rule it out. For the moment, though, I’m taking time to explore new interests and opportunities. I will keep my finger on the pulse and continue sharing my best insights with my readers.

I’m rooting for you, always.
